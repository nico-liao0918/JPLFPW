Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What’s included in the Basic Package?

A 4-page static website (Home, About, Contact, and one custom page of your choice). Hosting for 1 year is included via free platforms (e.g., GitHub Pages).

Q: How long will my project take?

Each project takes about 7 days to complete. This may vary depending on project queue. Rush orders are available at additional cost.

Q: What if I need more than 4 pages?

You can request an add-on service for each extra page or feature. These are billed separately.

Q: Can I request changes after I say it’s Final?

No further edits will be allowed after confirmation. However, additional edits can be done under the Flexi hourly package.
